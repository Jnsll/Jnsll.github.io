#### Lecture Title

_The Hidden Cost of AI: Toward Energy-Aware Software Engineering_

---

#### Abstract

As AI systems grow increasingly demanding, the environmental footprint of
software has become a systemic challenge for software engineering. Energy
consumption is shaped by the decisions engineers make at every stage of
development — from algorithm selection and model design to deployment and
inference strategies.

This lecture examines how software engineering decisions contribute to energy
consumption, how to reason about these costs, and what it means in practice to
embed sustainability as a first-class concern throughout the development
lifecycle.
